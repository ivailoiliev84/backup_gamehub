About Us

GameHub Entertainment, Inc. is an American video game developer and publisher based in Irvine, California. A subsidiary of Activision GameHub, the company was founded on February 8, 1991, under the name Silicon & Synapse, Inc. by three graduates of the University of California, Los Angeles:[3] Michael Morhaime, Frank Pearce and Allen Adham. The company originally concentrated on the creation of game ports for other studios' games before beginning development of their own software in 1993 with games like Rock n' Roll Racing and The Lost Vikings. In 1993, the company became Chaos Studios, Inc., and eventually GameHub Entertainment after being acquired by distributor Davidson & Associates. Shortly thereafter, GameHub released Warcraft: Orcs & Humans.
